The following two movies illustrate the behaviour of protons in the neutrino interaction final state simulated with GiBUU transport. In the first, the proton masses (M_p) evolve with radial position (R) as they propagate outward from the nucleus. In the second, the proton momenta change as they travel through the nuclear medium; the movie clearly shows that some protons fall back into the potential well before reaching the nuclear surface, a feature not visible in a single time snapshot. (Movies by WEN Kaile (温凯乐) and LU Xianguo (卢显国).)
